Introducing Keira

Keira is your personal mental health companion — an AI chatbot designed to support your well-being, anytime you need it. Whether you want to talk through your feelings or receive gentle affirmations, Keira is here to listen and guide you. Thoughtfully built to encourage mindfulness and emotional balance, Keira helps you take small, meaningful steps toward a calmer, healthier you.
